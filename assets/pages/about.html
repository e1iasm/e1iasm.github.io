About Me

I'm a data analyst and machine learning enthusiast based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, with a passion for transforming raw data into actionable insights. My journey into the world of data analysis began with a curious quest to support claims with tangible evidence. The lack of data-backed arguments at social gatherings often led to lively discussions, and I found myself constantly seeking the data to substantiate claims and uncover the truth.

This innate curiosity eventually led me to the Google Data Analytics course on Coursera, which opened my eyes to the vast potential of data analysis and its ability to inform decision-making. The course ignited a deep-seated passion for exploring the world of information and seeking answers through data-driven approaches.

To further hone my skills, I delved into hands-on projects, starting with guided ones that provided a structured framework for the entire process. These projects helped me grasp the intricacies of data analysis and visualization, laying a solid foundation for my own independent projects. My personal projects allowed me to explore topics that resonated with me, providing an opportunity to apply my skills in real-world scenarios. These projects also challenged me to think critically and navigate obstacles, fostering a growth mindset and resilience to overcome challenges.

The portfolio you're currently viewing showcases a selection of my most impactful projects, demonstrating my ability to extract valuable insights from data and translate them into clear and compelling visualizations. I'm proficient in various data analysis techniques, including data cleaning, data exploration, and statistical analysis.
