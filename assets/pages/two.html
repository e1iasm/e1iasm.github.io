Who rides the most?

Project Goal
To better understand the behavior of Citi Bike’s customer base (both one-time users and subscribers) and how they use Citi Bikes.

Excel features utilized:
● Pivot tables
● PivotTables
● Filtering and Sorting Data
● Excel Tables
● Functions

Key findings
● Top 5 pick-up locations for bikes:
○ Grove St Path, Exchange Place, Sip Ave, Hamilton Park, & Morris Canal
● Customer base
○ Most of the customers are between the ages 25-54 and are long-term subscribers
● Citi Bike customer behavior
○ 75+ customers take the longest trips on average while 65-74 take the shortest

Recommended actions
● Product recommendations<
○ Install more bikes at Grove St Path, Exchange Place, Sip Ave, Hamilton Park, & Morris Canal.
● Marketing recommendations
○ The Citi Bike customer base is mostly long-term subscribers aged between 25-64, who are most active on weekdays. This tells us that they are probably people who live in New York and use Citi Bikes to commute. Marketing and advertising campaigns should therefore target this particular demographic.
